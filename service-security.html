Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Common questions about our Security Services

Are security guards police verified?

Yes. All security guards undergo mandatory police and background verification before deployment to ensure trust, safety, and compliance.

Do you provide armed security guards?

Yes. Armed guards are deployed based on site-specific risk assessment, regulatory requirements, and client approval.

Is supervision provided at the site?

Yes. Dedicated supervisors and field officers regularly monitor security operations through inspections, audits, and reporting.

Can electronic security be integrated with guards?

Absolutely. We integrate trained security manpower with CCTV, access control, biometrics, and communication systems for enhanced, technology-enabled security.
