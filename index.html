Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries from HR Heads, Admin Managers, and Compliance Officers

How do you ensure compliance with changing labour laws?

We maintain a dedicated compliance team that monitors all labour law updates in real-time. Our platform automatically updates checklists and processes when new regulations are implemented. We provide quarterly compliance briefings to all clients and conduct internal audits to ensure adherence.

What is your approach to workforce scalability during peak seasons?

We maintain a reserve workforce pool of 20% above baseline requirements to handle seasonal spikes. Our elastic staffing model allows quick scaling within 72 hours notice. All reserve staff are pre-trained and compliance-verified, ensuring seamless integration without quality compromises.

How do you handle payroll for a multi-state workforce?

Our centralized payroll system automatically calculates state-specific statutory deductions (PF, ESI, professional tax, etc.) based on employee location. We maintain local compliance experts in each state to handle regional requirements. All payroll processing is automated with manual verification to ensure 100% accuracy.

What technology platform do you use for monitoring and reporting?

We use our proprietary platform, Wellworth FM Suite, which includes real-time attendance tracking, task management, compliance dashboards, and client portals. The platform is accessible via web and mobile apps, with role-based access for different stakeholders. All data is encrypted and stored in ISO 27001 certified data centers.

What is your incident response time for critical issues?

Our SLA guarantees a 15-minute response time for critical issues and 2-hour resolution for priority-1 incidents. We maintain 24/7 help desk support with escalation matrix to site managers, account directors, and if needed, executive leadership. All incidents are logged, tracked, and analyzed for preventive action.
