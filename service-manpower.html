Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Common questions about our Facility Management Services

Are FM staff on client payroll?

No. All Facility Management staff remain on Wellworth’s payroll, ensuring complete client indemnification from statutory and labour-related liabilities.

Do you provide supervisors and facility managers?

Yes. Dedicated supervisors and experienced facility managers are deployed based on site size and Services scope.

Can Facility Management Services be scaled up or down?

Yes. Our FM Services are flexible and scalable based on operational needs, business growth, or seasonal demand.

Do you ensure safety and hygiene compliance?

Absolutely. Health, safety, and hygiene are core pillars of our Facility Management Services, backed by SOPs and audits.
